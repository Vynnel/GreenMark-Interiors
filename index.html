    About Us

    GREENMARK INTERIORS LTD is an interior design studio.  We specialize in the design and development of residential and commercial projects. Either for a business or a residence, our interiors express our clients' objectives with an emphasis on maximum space functionality, meticulous and aesthetic sophistication.

    Services

    We approach each project first and foremost through a recognition by the the client, are taking a step towards making a difference in your spaces by hiring us.  Regardless of the scale or scope of a project, there's a great importance to working systematically.  Our process involves:

    Consultation

    We listen and learn about our clients' needs and desires.

    Inventory Our Clients' Belongings

    Catalogue existing belongings; and we may advise on retaining the essentials and discarding the superfluous.

    Schematic Design

    Conceptual space planning including floor plans, computer renderings, the selection of furnishings, finishes and accessories as needed to express the vision for clients' approval.

    Design Development

    Detailed drawings and specifications package which is actionable by vendors and trades and fundamental to a project's successful execution.

    Purchasing

    Ordering of furnishings, finishes and accessories required to produce a turnkey product or simply execute the scope of work.

    Project Coordination

    Bidding, scheduling, liaising with vendors, trades and service providers, code consultants, architects, and all the human resources involved in bringing a new construction or a renovation project to completion on time and on budget.
